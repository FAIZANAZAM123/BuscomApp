About Caceis

Our product portfolio encompasses real-time market data, index data from the DAX and STOXX indices, as well as reference data and historical data. In addition, we use the highly granular market data from Eurex and Xetra to develop meaningful analytics that help our clients optimise their trading strategies. Our growing offering includes tools such as Xetra and Eurex Flow Insights, Eurex Open Interest Insights or HHI Insights, all of which can be obtained directly from our Data Shop. Our cloud-based analytics platform A7 covers historical market data from CME Group as well as historical and intraday Data from Eurex, Xetra and EEX.
